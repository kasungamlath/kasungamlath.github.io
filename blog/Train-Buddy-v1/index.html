Train Buddy v1

Published on 2015-04-17

After few months of beta testing, today I have released the Train Buddy app to public. Train Buddy is a android app for getting the train schedule of Srilankan trains and view the current status (location and speed) of the train. You can learn more from the Google play app description.

If you are interested in testing the future versions of app please join this community page. Members of this community will always get the latest functions first.
