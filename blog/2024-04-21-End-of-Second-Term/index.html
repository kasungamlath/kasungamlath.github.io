End of Second Term

Published on 2024-04-21

Past week was the first exam week (exams usually held over two weeks) for the second term of 23-24 academic year. Exams for both of the courses I was supporting, and the course I was reading for the masters, held during last week. So (except for few grading components) term is over for me.

For the record I was supporting CS205-Operating Systems with Android and IS113-Web Application development 1. And I was reading the CS704-Information Systems.
