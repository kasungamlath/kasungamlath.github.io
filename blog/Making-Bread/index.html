Making Bread

Published on 2024-03-19

Early this month I made bread. Following are the ingredients and process recommended by (my baking instructor) TOM.

Ingredients

  • wheat Floor - 300g
  • water - 150g
  • sugar - 30g
  • yeast - 6g
  • salt - to taste
  • honey - 1 tea spoon

Process

  1. Mix all the ingreadients together. Knead it. Leave it for ~2 hours.
  2. Knead the mixture again. Leave it for ~30 min.
  3. Make the structure of bread.
  4. Leave it for ~30 min.

Mine looked like this.

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C.
  2. Bake the bread for ~25 min.
  3. Take the bread out and let it cool in room tempreture.
