2023 So Far

Published on 2023-02-19

January was all rain. That means no cycling, no Tennis, only an occasional run.

I did,

  • bench press - 30 x 5, 35 x 5, 40 x 5, 30 x 10 x 5
  • back squat - 45 x 5, 50 x 5, 55 x 5, 40 x 10 x 5
  • dead lift - 60 x 5, 70 x 5, 85 x 5, 50 x 10 x 5

For whatever reason squats hit me the hardest and I had trouble climbing stairs even after 2 days of work out.
