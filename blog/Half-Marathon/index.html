Half Marathon

Published on 2020-12-14

Early this month I ran a half marathon. Though I did it without stoping it took almost 3 hours. Average pace is 8 min per kilo meter. For the context world record for the marathon is just under 2 hours.

But still I feel this is a long way since me celebrating doing a (almost)5k in just 6 months ago.

This will be the longest run I'm doing in 2020. I need to improve the pace before doing another.

I also met a huge wild boar during this run.
