Flying Home

Published on 2022-09-29

I never liked flying. I hate the waiting until the flight. Flying itself is not so bad. Once I get in to the plane, It's all good. I just don't like the waiting. This is the second time this year I'm flying. First time was more interesting. First time this year was the first time I flew since covid. After about a 2 years wait. I think it gets little better each time. Meaning if I fly more frequently I'll feel better. But the issue is I'm also not very keen on flying. Travel by train - well that's a way of traveling I like. Issue with flying is, there's nothing to the journey. There's no scenery. You hop on a plane and try to kill the time until you get off. And the hassle of checking in and out. Granted - It's (unfortunately) the fastest way to travel.
