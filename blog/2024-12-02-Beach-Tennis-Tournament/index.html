Beach Tennis Tournament

Published on 2024-12-02

I Joined my first Beach Tennis Tournament this November. And played in the mixed double category.

We won 1 out of 4 games. And we almost lost the game we won. We were down to 0-4 at one point. The final score for winning game is 7-5.

For the rest it's 0-6, 1-6 and, 4-6.

I was quite stressed during the tournament. And found hitting the ball harder than I usually do, causing it to go either long or wide. The game felt way harder and tiring than when I play for fun.

Now I have a IFBT ranking. 2 to be precise. Though I've no idea why there are 2 rankings.
