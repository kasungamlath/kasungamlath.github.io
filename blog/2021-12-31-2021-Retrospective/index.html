2021 Retrospective

Published on 2021-12-31

Today is the final day of 2021. It's being a quite interesting year, to say the least. Mostly stuck at home. Pretty sure I was depressed, at least slightly, in the first part of the year. Apart from that, in year 2021 I, * Shot, processed and published 32 sets of [photos](/photos). * Wrote (whole lot of) 1 blog post. * Bought a GoPro, with plans to building a documentary of my day-to-day life. * Build a gaming PC, which was a childhood dream and will facilitate editing videos/photos. * Set up a home theater with a projector+roku stick and a media server. * Bought a Garmin Fenix 6, to help my running training. * Run 687km this year. ![full-width](/assets/running.png) * Including 2 races. In-person 5k ![full-width](/assets/2021_5k.png) And a virtual half marathon. ![full-width](/assets/2021_half_marathon.png) * Cycled 462km ![full-width](/assets/cycling.png) So again, It's being an interesting year.
