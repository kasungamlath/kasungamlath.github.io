Challah Bread

Published on 2024-03-19

Early this month I made Challah bread (for the first time). Following are the ingredients and process recommended by (my baking instructor) TOM. ### Ingredients * wheat Floor - 300g * water - 150g * sugar - 30g * yeast - 6g * salt - to taste * honey - 1 tea spoon ### Process 1. Mix all the ingreadients together. Knead it. Leave it for ~2 hours. 2. Knead the mixture again. Leave it for ~30 min. 3. Make the structure of bread. 4. Leave it for ~30 min. Mine looked like this. ![full-width](/assets/bread/before.jpg) 5. Preheat the oven to 180C. 6. Bake the bread for ~25 min. 7. Take the bread out and let it cool in room tempreture. Final result looked like this. ![full-width](/assets/bread/after.jpg) Apart from having not enough salt, It tasted like bread.
