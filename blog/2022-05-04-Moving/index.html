Moving

Published on 2022-05-04

May 1st 2022 I moved to a new place. I've being at my previous place for 2+ years. It was a rented HDB flat at Houagang, with some of my friends. During my stay rent was changed between 400 - 1100 SGD. Depending on the number of tenants at the period and the house rent. The last rent I paid was 2400SGD/5 = 480SGD

My new place is a common room at Condominium in Aljunied. New place costs 1300SGD - 50 = 1250SGD. So it's roughly 3x of the old price.

I managed to pack all my belongings to 1 checking luggage plus 2 hand luggages. Except for the PC and 3 pair of shoes. 1 hand luggage is filled with almost useless stuff. Should clear it sometime.
