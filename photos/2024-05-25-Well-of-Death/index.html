Well of Death

Published on 2024-05-25

"Well of Death". Stuntmen performing inside the structure and spectators are watching from the top. Shot at Wattarama Temple, Sri Lanka during the Vesak festival 2024. To be clear this has nothing to do with Vesak but a common occurrence (among other festivities) during each Vesak season in this temple (Since my childhood days).
