Pesta Sukan Athletics

Published on 2023-08-05

This time it was Athletics in Pesta Sukan 2023. I signed up for 5-10pm shift. Hoping to avoid the heat. Unfortunately the events ended at 5:30pm. So didn't get a much chance to shoot. Only caught the final jump of the high jump (by [Michelle Sng)[https://www.instagram.com/michsng/] and 20x200 event. Michelle Sng, She holds the Singapore National record for Female High Jump.
