Coney Island

Published on 2020-03-14

Initial plan was to ride the bicycle to Coney Island. But rent was 8$ per hour. So decided to go by bus as fast as possible and walk the rest. Ended up walking about 2km. In the island there wasn't much to see. May be because of the route we chose. Only met few birds and butterflies from animal department. Also the island was full of one kind of a tree.
