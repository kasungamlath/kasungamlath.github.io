Southern Ridges Walk

Published on 2024-06-09

Today (Sunday), I joined my friend's weekend hiking group to go for a walk in Southern Ridges. I remember doing this route sometime back, but couldn't find any record.

We started around 8am. Got caught in a light rain somewhere in the middle, for about 20 min.

The scenery was green with occasional glimpses of buildings. The weather was humid (today it's more humid than Singapore usual), and we were drenched with sweat.

We walked for about 10 km, and finished the walk around 11am. The lunch was at a food court in Buona-Vista. In the food court, there's a famous chicken rice stall with a long queue. Queue was too long for me, so I had some fried chicken rice from another stall, and took a bus home.
