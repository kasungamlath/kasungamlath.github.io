C2C Bike Ride

Published on 2020-10-24

There is a trail in Singapore called Coast to Coast(C2C) trail. I went on it by bike. I was not using the GPS in the phone and relying on a pdf map. (I am trying to cut down the dependency on the mobile phone for some time now) By the time I was little bit farther than the mid point(Macritchie Reservoir), I have already travelled past 40km. Total distance of the trail is 36km. Which tells lot about my map reading skills. And it was around 4 pm already, So I turned back and came home.
