C2C Bike Ride

Published on 2020-10-24

There is a trail in Singapore called [Coast to Coast(C2C) trail](https://www.nparks.gov.sg/gardens-parks-and-nature/parks-and-nature-reserves/coast-to-coast). I went on it by bike. I was not using the GPS in the phone and relying on a pdf map. (I am trying to cut down the dependency on the mobile phone for some time now) By the time I was little bit farther than the mid point(Macritchie Reservoir), I have already travelled past 40km. Total distance of the trail is 36km. Which tells lot about my map reading skills.
