Labrador to Harbourfront Photo Walk

Published on 2022-11-05

First photo walk in the November. Rainy day. Was quite a challenge to wake up in the morning. Only two others show up for the walk. Didn't meet many people nor birds during. Had a long discussion about jobs and life, while drinking a chocolate with marshmallow at the end of the walk.
