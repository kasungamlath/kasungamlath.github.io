Morning in the Pungol Park

Published on 2020-03-21

Went for a walk in the park in the morning with plans to shoot some birds. Unfortunately most of the bird shots are out of focus. The reason being it's difficulty of getting the auto focus to work on birds due to obstacals(branches, twigs, leaves, etc..) in line of site. When tried with manual focus, bird usually flies away before getting it locked. :(
