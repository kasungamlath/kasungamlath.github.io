Lake Round Kurunegala On A Rainy Day

Published on 2024-05-20

It was raining since I landed in Sri Lanka. I landed on Friday and today is Monday. (Only Saturday morning it didn't rain) So decided to go out today even it is raining (wanted to meet a friend too). Colors were bland for most of the shots due to dark weather, so monochrome it is.
