Trip to Riverston

Published on 2022-10-22

This time it's a trip to Riverston. Located in Matale, Sri Lanka. 3+ hours drive from home and last 20km road is pretty bad. It was super misty. Couldn't see the sun at all. Wasn't much people around either. But thankfully it didn't rain and was able to took some misty photos.
