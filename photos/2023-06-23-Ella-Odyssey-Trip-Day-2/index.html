Ella Odyssey Trip Day 2

Published on 2023-06-23

Second day of the odyssey trip. We got up early from the place we stayed at Ella and took an early train to Haputhale. Then we rented two bikes from Haputhale and started the bike part of the journey. Took this when we were passing the Ambewela railway station.
