SGP vs THA FIFA2026 Qualifiers

Published on 2023-11-21

Shooting a game from pitch side. This time, I felt like an amature. I didn't bring a portable stool, nor mono-pod and my lens is not long enough (but with my 70-200mm I was still able to cover a half of the court). And I was among the few who brought only one camera. But it was a great experience (and photos came out not so bad either).
