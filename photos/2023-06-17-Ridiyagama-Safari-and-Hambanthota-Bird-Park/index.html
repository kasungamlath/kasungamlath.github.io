Ridiyagama Safari and Hambanthota Bird Park

Published on 2023-06-17

Second day of the family trip. After the breakfast and another car fix up we visited the Ridiyagama safari. Where you can travel among free moving animals in a covered vehicle. I liked the way this small deer is lighted up.
